Pineapple Grove Designs' new website makes it easy for designers to explore all the Architectural Art the company has to offer. Collection and Custom items in sculpted panels, tiles and grilles in engineered cast stone, polymer composite and architectural metal are illustrated in easy to navigate galleries with accompanying details and specifications.

Over 28,000 architects and designers have specified and commissioned Pineapple Grove Designs' products since 1989. All work is designed and fabricated in the USA from locally sourced material and shipped directly to the customer's project.

Pineapple Grove Designs provides exceptional products and service for private, institutional, commercial and government projects throughout North America.
